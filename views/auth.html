UITH Physiotherapy Scheduling System

Welcome to the Department of Physiotherapy scheduling system at University of Ilorin Teaching Hospital. This platform helps manage duty schedules, shift requests, and staff assignments efficiently.

Our system ensures fair distribution of workload, streamlines schedule management, and facilitates clear communication among staff members.
